About

Hi, I'm Pete. This is my portfolio of interesting work. I'm an amicable guy who will listen to you, understand your needs, and craft a great solution for you. With my mechanical engineering background I know quality and precision. For me, clear and clean function comes before form, and has an aesthetic all its own. I'm no robot though - your solution will be both working and beautiful.

Aside from this web fun, I'm constantly intrigued and inspired by all aspects of life - from anthropology, climate change and biology through to other people, surfing, relationships and mindfulness. I find joy in creation, whether it be building websites, growing tomatoes or making music.

I'm currently based in Wellington, New Zealand.
